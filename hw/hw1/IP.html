The Internet Protocol(IP)

IP or Internet Protocol is the standard for which machines over the internet or any network forward or route packets based on their IP address.

Routing

Routing is one of the most important functions of IP. Routing is the process of forwarding IP packets from its source to its destination over a network by finding the best route through different nodes and routers.

Addressing

IP Addresses are unique numbers identifying a machine whether it be a school computer, router, server, or even your phone. Just like a mail carrier needs your address to ship packages, so does IP/TCP need your ip address to send packets.
